The Starbucks experience

Make it your own - Make an emotional connection, with welcoming and genuine personal interactions displaying awareness, kindness and authenticity.

Everything matters - Nothing is trivial, people notice everything. You can't hide poor service or poor quality.

Surprise and delight - Service is an expectation! Anticipate guests needs, provide comfort, earn trust; own your mistakes and go all in to rectify any misses.

Embrace resistance - Criticism presents a unique opportunity to improve upon current practices to become better at your own game. ALWAYS be graceful when addressing grievances.

Lave your mark - Develop a reputation of integrity and social responsibility where trust, the community and the environment are an unwavering goal while consciousness and diversity drive the efforts of giving back.
